About Naked Coffee:

Naked Coffee is not just about coffee. Not just a restaurant, not just a roastery, not just a music venue. We are, of course, all of those things and more, but Naked Coffee is essentially an idea, a concept, a goal. Naked Coffee is a vision, and perhaps an unattainable one, but a genuine vision that, nonetheless, every one of us believes is still worth pursuing.

Naked Coffee has been a continuous evolution, developed through trial and error, carefully considering every transition, overcoming our fair share of detours, setbacks, and obstacles. Naked Coffee has grown slowly, thoughtfully, organically, relying upon every individual who has chosen to be a part of Naked Coffee.

Each of us with our own unique gifts and idiosyncrasies, our own strengths and weaknesses, our own trials and triumphs, and leaving our own indelible mark on the history of Naked Coffee.
